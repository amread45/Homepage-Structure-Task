Understanding Web Accessibility

By Jane Doe
May 8, 2025

Web accessibility ensures that websites are usable by people of all abilities and disabilities. It's an essential aspect of web development.

Here are some tools that can help test web accessibility:

  • WAVE by WebAIM
  • axe Accessibility Checker
  • Google Lighthouse
  • NVDA Screen Reader

To learn more about accessibility, visit W3C Web Accessibility Initiative.
